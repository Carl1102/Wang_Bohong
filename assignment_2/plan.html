Goal

The goal of the 100SMILES Dental Care website is to increase new and returning patient enquiries and confirmed bookings in Razole. The site will do this by:

  • Clearly explaining the range of services for children, adults, and seniors.
  • Highlighting promotions such as the free dental check-up day and discounts on the Annual Dental Check-Up package.
  • Making it easy to contact the clinic and submit an online booking request at any time.
  • Reassuring visitors through staff profiles, clinic images, and descriptions of advanced dental technologies.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website will be evaluated using both enquiry and engagement measures:

  • The number of online booking form submissions per month.
  • The number of phone calls and email enquiries that mention the website or online form.
  • Web analytics data (for example, from Google Analytics), including:
    • Total visits and proportion of new visitors.
    • Average time spent on the Services and Contact pages.
    • Click-through rate on key calls-to-action such as “Book an appointment”.
    • Reduction in bounce rate on the homepage after improvements.
  • The number of patients using promotions advertised on the site (free check-up day, Annual Check-Up discount).

Target Audience

The primary target audience is people living in Razole and nearby areas who require general or specialist dental care. This includes:

  • Parents with children who need regular check-ups, preventive care, and comfortable appointments with a pediatric dentist.
  • Busy working adults looking for convenient booking options, clear information about treatments, and pricing or promotional offers.
  • Elderly patients and carers who may require restorative or ongoing dental treatment and who need reassurance about gentle, long-term care.

The design therefore focuses on clear navigation, readable text, friendly images of staff and patients, and obvious calls-to-action so that all age groups can quickly find what they need and feel confident to book.
