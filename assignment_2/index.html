About 100SMILES Dental Care

100SMILES Dental Care has served the Razole community for more than thirty years at 33 Fantail Street. Our team brings decades of combined experience and focuses on reliable, caring treatment for every patient.

We provide a full range of dental care – from routine check-ups and cleaning through to implants, orthodontic treatment, and cosmetic options. Patients can also access emergency appointments, diagnostic services, and guidance to help them look after their teeth at home.

Whether you are booking for a child, yourself, or an elderly family member, we aim to make every visit comfortable and stress free.

Advanced Dental Technology

Modern equipment helps us diagnose and treat problems more accurately and comfortably.

Digital X-Rays

Digital X-rays provide clear images instantly with less radiation than traditional film-based X-rays. They support early detection and gentle care.

Intraoral Imaging & Teledentistry

High-resolution intraoral cameras and secure video consultations help you understand treatment options and access advice from home when appropriate.

CAD/CAM & 3D Printing

Computer-aided design and 3D printing allow us to create accurate crowns, bridges, surgical guides, and custom appliances with an excellent fit.

Gentle Restorative Care

Detailed imaging and planning mean that restorative treatments can be completed with precision, focusing on comfort and long-term results.
