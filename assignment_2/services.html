Our Dental Services

From routine care to advanced treatment, we provide a wide range of services for patients of all ages in Razole and the surrounding area.

General & Preventive Care

  • Routine dental check-ups and examinations
  • Professional teeth cleaning and scaling
  • Oral health and nutrition advice
  • X-rays and other diagnostic services
  • Children’s dentistry and preventive care

Restorative & Specialist Care

  • Fillings, crowns, and bridges
  • Dental implants and implant-retained dentures
  • Root canal treatment
  • Orthodontic treatment, including braces and aligners
  • Cosmetic dentistry – whitening and veneers

Annual Dental Check-Up Package

Our Annual Dental Check-Up is a bundled visit designed to review your overall oral health and catch any potential problems early.

  • Full oral examination and discussion of any concerns
  • Professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar
  • Digital X-rays where clinically appropriate
  • Assessment for common dental diseases and personalised care advice
  • Guidance on diet and lifestyle to support healthy teeth and gums
  • Additional tests if recommended based on your age and dental history

Our New Dental Technologies

These tools help us provide more precise, comfortable, and efficient treatment.

Digital X-Rays

Clear images with significantly lower radiation levels than traditional film.

Intraoral Cameras

Tiny cameras allow us to show you detailed views of your teeth and gums so you can see exactly what we see during your appointment.

Laser Dentistry

Laser tools assist with selected treatments, helping reduce discomfort and improving healing in suitable cases.

CAD/CAM & 3D Printing

Digital design and 3D printing are used to create crowns, bridges, and guides with a precise fit.

3D Planning & Surgical Guides

3D planning tools assist in complex restorative and implant procedures for safe, accurate placement.

Teledentistry

Secure online consultations make it easier to discuss concerns and receive professional advice without always needing an in-person visit.
