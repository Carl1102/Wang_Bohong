Meet Our Team

Our friendly team of dentists, hygienists, and support staff work together to provide care for children, adults, and seniors in a calm, welcoming environment.

Dr Johanna Grey

DDS, Orthodontics

Dr Grey focuses on orthodontic care, helping patients of all ages straighten their teeth and improve their bite. She enjoys designing treatment plans that fit each person’s lifestyle.

Dr Emily Carter

Pediatric Dentist

Dr Carter is dedicated to children’s dental health. Her gentle approach and clear explanations help children feel relaxed and confident during their visits.

Dr Benedict Sloan

DMD

Dr Sloan brings many years of clinical experience to 100SMILES. He focuses on long-term oral health and works closely with patients to plan appropriate treatment.

Richard Attam

Dental Hygienist

Richard supports our dentists by providing thorough cleaning and preventive care. He enjoys helping patients develop home-care routines that keep their teeth and gums healthy.

April Kepner

Receptionist

April is often the first smiling face you will see or hear when you contact the clinic. She manages appointments and enquiries to keep your visit running smoothly.
